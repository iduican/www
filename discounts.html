Alternative Care Services

Alternative Care Services 10% to 30% off

Save 10% to 30% on non-covered alternative care services from providers participating in the Premera Discount program.

Use our Find a Doctor feature for easy access to a network of contracted and credentialed alternative care providers in Washington.

Alternative Care Services Disciplines

The discount is applicable to the following professional services when performed by participating practitioners:

  • Chiropractic: Discounts apply to all chiropractic services.
  • Licensed Acupuncturists: Discounts include all non-covered professional acupuncturist services.
  • Licensed Massage Therapists: Discount includes all non-covered professional massage therapy services.
  • Naturopathic Physicians: Discount includes non-covered services such as wellness visits, consultations, and physical medicine treatments.
  • Dietetic and nutrition services

How to Use the Discount

  • Call your selected practitioner to make an appointment. Let the practitioner know you want to use the Extras! Discount and describe which scenario applies to you:
  • Services are not covered under your medical plan (e.g. massage therapy)
  • Services are covered under your medical plan, but you’ve exceeded your maximum benefit coverage (e.g. you’ve reached your allowed dollar limit for the year and want to continue services)
  • Present your plan ID card at the time of your appointment.
  • Pay less with the Extras! Alternative Care Services discount program. Pay the provider’s billed charges, less the program discount. You are responsible for the cost of all services and products that you receive from a participating practitioner through this program.

Please note: Discounts apply immediately to complementary and alternative provider services that are not covered by your health plan.
